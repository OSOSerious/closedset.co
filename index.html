What is ClosedSet.ai?

ClosedSet.ai is an innovative solution developed by an intimacy coordinator and a tech expert specializing in blockchain and AI. It provides robust security for sensitive production data, ensuring protection for both studios and talent.

Our Mission

ClosedSet aims to create systems of integrity by using blockchain steganography to protect sensitive production information, ensuring online security for both talent and studios.

Goals & Strategy

  • Protect Talent: Safeguard images and continuity footage, especially for intimate and/or nude scenes.
  • Blockchain Security: Every individual who views or shares information has their own transaction wallet, allowing all transactions to be tracked and logged.
  • Expiration Management: Information shared automatically expires in 72 hours, but talent or studios can extend the expiration in 48-hour increments.

Blockchain Technology and Online Safety

ClosedSet utilizes blockchain to ensure security for sensitive production information. Every transaction involving sensitive data is encoded and embedded with a unique wallet ID, making it trackable and identifiable across various platforms. The system is designed to be used across devices for both talent and studios, ensuring seamless protection regardless of the technology employed.

  • Continuity Footage
  • Talent Images
  • Body and/or Stunt Doubles
  • Intimate Scenes: Initially developed for the protection of sensitive materials like nude photos for casting, but can also be applied to contracts, NDAs, script changes, and personnel info.

Technical Pathway

  1. Upload Content: Talent or production uploads sensitive data.
  2. Embed with Wallet Info: Data is embedded with individual wallet information.
  3. Encryption: Data is encrypted and shared with necessary parties.
  4. Tracking and Identifying: Each interaction with the content is tracked and identifiable.
  5. Secure Sharing: Data is protected from unauthorized online platforms.

Goals for Online Safety

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

1. Overview

This SOP outlines the procedures and guidelines for operating and maintaining the ClosedSet.co platform. The objective is to ensure consistency, efficiency, and security in all business operations.

2. Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to provide clear instructions and standardized processes to ensure that all team members perform their tasks effectively and in alignment with the companyâ€™s goals.

3. Scope

This SOP applies to all employees, contractors, and partners involved in the development, maintenance, support, and management of the ClosedSet.co platform.

4. Definitions

  • Platform: The ClosedSet.co online security solution.
  • Stakeholders: Employees, contractors, partners, and clients using the platform.
  • Security Protocols: Measures implemented to protect data integrity and confidentiality.

5. Procedures

5.1. Platform Development

  1. Use version control systems (e.g., Git) for all code repositories.
  2. Conduct regular code reviews to ensure quality and security standards.
  3. Implement continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
  4. Maintain comprehensive documentation for all development processes.

5.2. Data Security

  1. Encrypt all sensitive data using industry-standard encryption algorithms.
  2. Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all user accounts.
  3. Regularly update and patch all software components to protect against vulnerabilities.
  4. Conduct periodic security audits and penetration testing.

5.3. Customer Support

  1. Provide multiple support channels (email, chat, phone) for customer inquiries.
  2. Respond to all support requests within 24 hours.
  3. Maintain a comprehensive knowledge base and FAQ section on the website.
  4. Collect and analyze customer feedback to improve the platform.

5.4. Marketing and Sales

  1. Develop targeted marketing campaigns to reach key customer segments.
  2. Utilize SEO and SEM strategies to increase online visibility.
  3. Attend industry conferences and events to network and promote the platform.
  4. Implement a referral program to encourage word-of-mouth marketing.

5.5. Partnership Management

  1. Identify and establish partnerships with key industry players.
  2. Negotiate mutually beneficial agreements with partners.
  3. Maintain regular communication with partners to ensure alignment.
  4. Evaluate and optimize partnership performance periodically.

6. Responsibilities

  • Development Team: Responsible for building and maintaining the platform.
  • Security Team: Ensures data protection and platform security.
  • Customer Support Team: Handles user inquiries and issues.
  • Marketing Team: Drives brand awareness and customer acquisition.
  • Partnership Managers: Develop and maintain strategic partnerships.

7. Documentation

  • Maintain up-to-date documentation for all processes and procedures.
  • Ensure all team members have access to relevant documents.
  • Regularly review and update documentation to reflect current practices.

8. Review and Update Process
